Key Insights Guide

  • The dashboard begins with KPI cards to summarise the selected year and countries.
  • The literacy section provides an overview of education outcomes through trend and map-based views.
  • The access and investment section compares funding, tertiary enrolment, and enrolment across education levels.
  • The relationship section uses a heatmap to identify which education indicators move together.
  • The navigation buttons allow users to move between sections while keeping the dashboard as one cohesive page.
